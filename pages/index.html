About Pura

Serious about movement.
Relaxed about everything else.

Built for people who want to move well — without the ego, the hustle, or the corporate wellness vibe.

Pura Pilates is a boutique studio on Sri Lanka's south coast. Small group reformer and mat classes, private sessions, and retreat programmes. The community here is a mix — surfers, travellers, locals, remote workers. Everyone welcome. Ocean outside. Vibe warm.
